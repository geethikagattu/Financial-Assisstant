The SMART Goals Framework

Specific

What exactly do you want to achieve?

Measurable

How will you track your progress?

Achievable

Is your goal realistic given your resources?

Relevant

Does this goal align with your values?

Time-Bound

When do you want to achieve this goal?

Examples of SMART Goals

Instead of saying "I want to save money," a SMART goal would be: "I will save $500 for a new laptop by setting aside $50 from my paycheck every month for the next 10 months."
