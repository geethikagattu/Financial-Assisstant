Simple Methods to Monitor Your Money

The App Method

Use a modern budgeting app like Mint or YNAB. These apps link to your bank accounts and automatically categorize your spending, giving you a clear overview of your habits.

The Spreadsheet Method

For a hands-on approach, use a simple spreadsheet. Manually enter your income and expenses each day or week to keep a detailed record and stay aware of every dollar.

Why Tracking is Important

Tracking your spending is the foundation of any good budget. It helps you identify where your money is going, find hidden areas to save, and gives you a realistic view of your financial health.
